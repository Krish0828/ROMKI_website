Food

The Capturing the atmosphere of its absolute ocean front location, “Seafood” offers dining either at the outdoor beachfront terrace under the palms or in the cool, relaxed interiors of the indoor restaurant. Fresh blue and white tones and louvered shutters emote relaxed, sophisticated oceanfront dining. The focus is on simple, beautifully prepared and presented fresh produce from around the globe. Our Chef chooses the freshest seafood from Phuket’s markets, along with the very best flown in from around the world. Open daily for dinner from 6pm – 10.30pm
