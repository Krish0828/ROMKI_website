Experience Kayaking!

April 03, 2023
by: Juan De La Cruz

Paddle Asia specializes in intimate small group sea kayaking, jungle kayaking, jungle trekking, jungle survival, bushcraft (camping skills), mountain biking, and outdoor adventure travel tours to remote and unspoiled destinations in Southern Thailand. It is our mission to offer you something completely different from what other tour operators in the region offer. We only run small group tours! Most tours are two to four guests. Our max is eight.

Your kayaking tours are designed for first-time paddlers and novice travelers, and children are more than welcome! Experienced kayakers and sports persons are, of course, very welcome. You paddle your own kayak.

Very young or folks who are unable to paddle for themselves can have a guide paddle with them in a double kayak. Immerse yourself in natural beauty kayaking or other outdoor programs that are right for you. We are dedicated wildlife, bird, and nature lovers. Frequently our tours include sightings of rare or endangered species such as Sun Bears, Malayan Tapirs, Serows, and wild Elephants; always a delight to our guests and our guides.
