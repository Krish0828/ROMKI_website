ROOMS

 

PREMIER GARDEN VIEW ROOM

Located on the third floor with an elevated vista across the stunning indigenous gardens of this Koh Samui 5 star resort, Premier Garden View rooms offer a charming island haven. Enjoy the option of a sumptuous king size bed or twin beds that are dressed in the finest linens, as well as a host of luxury creature comforts. Relax on the cosy sofa on your balcony and soak up the magic of tropical island life.

DELUXE GARDEN VIEW ROOM

Positioned on the second floor of our resort in Samui, the Deluxe Garden View rooms offer a spacious sanctuary with the choice of a king size or twin beds, and the convenient option of requesting an extra bed. Slide open the floor to ceiling glass doors and relax on your cosy sofa which is just outside in the open air. Then gaze over the lush tropical grounds of this Samui resort and spa.

DELUX SEA VIEW ROOM

Drink in a panorama of Bophut Beach and the Gulf of Siam’s tranquil waters from the comfort on your outdoor sofa, while enjoying the view from the second or third floors of this luxurious resort & spa in Samui. Inside, Deluxe Sea View rooms are richly decorated to reflect our resort’s enchanting location. Refresh under the cascade of your rain shower, and rest in a bedroom of southern Thai inspired artwork and rich silks.
